Overview

You can delete your account and related data directly in the app. Deletion removes your Firebase Authentication account and your user document stored in Firestore. Push notification tokens linked to your account are invalidated.

How to Delete Your Account (In‑App)

  1. Open the app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Note: Deletion is immediate once you confirm.

Data Types Deleted

  • Account identifiers (email, user ID)
  • User profile document and app-related settings owned by the account
  • Push notification token associated with the account

Retention

Deletion occurs immediately upon confirmation. Residual copies may persist temporarily in provider-level backups and are removed according to the provider’s standard retention schedules. We do not retain your data for other purposes.

Delete Without Logging In

If you cannot access the app, email dev.geeyong@gmail.com with the subject Account Deletion Request and include the email address used in the app. We will verify ownership and process deletion.

Partial Data Deletion

Not supported at this time. Please request full account deletion if needed.
